Why Live Strings are the Better Choice

Sure, you could pay a band to play your favorite songs. But then the heat is already turned up. Where do you go from there for your reception?

Strings have always been and will always be intimate and romantic. It creates a sacred space for you and yours truly.

Save the party for afterwards, and walk down the aisle in a classic ambiance, while listening to the favorite tunes in a new light.

String Quartet

This ensemble provides the fullest sound with rich harmonies to support the melody. This ensemble is often comprised of 2 violins, 1 viola, and 1 cello.

String Trio

This ensemble continues to provide a full sound with rich harmonies supporting the melody. This ensemble is often comprised of 1 violins, 1 viola, and 1 cello.

String Duet

This beautiful ensemble is a simplification of the basic elements of music, melody and harmony. This ensemble is often comprised of 1 violin and 1 cello.

String Solo

This is the most basic ensemble of music, composed of the melody alone. This ensemble can be comprised of any instrument.
